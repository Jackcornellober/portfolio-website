Intro

At Lambda school, we will become full stack web developers in less than a year. So far we've created webpages based on design photos and learned to make their layouts responsive to the dimensions of the screen they're being viewed on. Check it out!.

During my time at lambda school, I look forward to learning the ins and outs of Javascript more than anything else. I always enjoyed mathematics in school, and so far Javascript seems similar to math. There are many paths to arrive at any given end result, and one detail out of place ruins everything!

Work

For our first project, we turned a few bits of plain text into this using HTML and CSS.

Since then we've added more pages and applied new techniques to that same website such as spacing its elements using Flexbox, and most recently utilized media queries to ensure our content could be smoothly viewed from tablets and mobile devices.

About

Outside of programming, my passions are performance art and gaming. I've spent time on the pro scene of the card game "Magic", and I've performed fire arts and juggling in backyards, on stages, in schools, and for audiences as large as 100,000 people. I've learned a lot from my passions, and I now aim to find out what I can do when I put the same effort into web development.
